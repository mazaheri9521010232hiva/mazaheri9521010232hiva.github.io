What is graphic design?

"The details are not the details. They make the design." These words about the nature of the job, expressed by Charles Eames, have become a well-known eternal truth for graphic designers. They are actually the people buliding a bridge between the art and the functional goal centered design. They mix the power of art and functionality within the limits of particular goals and a definite audience. They make beauty word for people.Here in this article, we have felt all the variety of stones in that way.
